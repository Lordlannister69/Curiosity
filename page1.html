Who created Curiosity?

Curiosity's exploration of Mars is the mission of this Mars Science Laboratory astromobile, which was developed by NASA and landed on Mars in Gale Crater on August 6, 2012.


What can it do?

Curiosity is a robot which can take results about water, this machine is waterproof and has a really strong resilience system, which allows it to repair itself with a Plasma gun. This robot can also take care of itself with a recharge adapted to the planet’s resources, and an AI which makes it autonomous.
